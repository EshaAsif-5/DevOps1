Tip Calculator

Quickly split a bill with tip and round the totals neatly.

Enter values and click Calculate.

How it works

We compute: tip = bill × (tip% / 100). Then: total = bill + tip. Per-person: total / people. Values are rounded to 2 decimals.

Pro tip: change the default percentages in the HTML if your region uses different norms.

Unit Converter

Centimeters ⇄ Inches (handy for quick size checks).

Enter a value and convert either way.

Conversions used

1 inch = 2.54 cm. We round to 3 decimals for inches and 2 decimals for centimeters.

Extend by duplicating this card and changing formulas (e.g., kg ⇄ lb, °C ⇄ °F).
